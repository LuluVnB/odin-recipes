Description:

It's an In-n-Out Double Double with lettuce, tomatoes, beef patties, buns, and cheese

Ingredients:

  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • beef patties
  • cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the beef patties
  2. Cook the tomatoes
  3. Put the beef patties and tomatoes in between two buns
  4. Put the cheese on top of the patties while they are still hot
  5. Enjoy!
